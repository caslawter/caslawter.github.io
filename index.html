Squid Game Salesman appreciation club

This is the ultimate gathering spot for fans who admire the enigmatic charm, persuasive skills, and mysterious aura of the Squid Game Salesman. Whether it’s his signature slap game, iconic business card, or his ability to recruit with a calm yet compelling demeanor, we’re here to celebrate everything that makes him unforgettable!

First impression

From the moment he steps into the scene with his slick suit and calm demeanor, you can’t help but be drawn to him. His composed and professional vibe instantly sets him apart, making him a fascinating character to watch.

His Kindness

The Salesman never forces anyone to play the game. He presents his offer calmly and with respect, treating the participants as individuals capable of making their own choices. Unlike the masked guards or game controllers, his manner feels personal and non-threatening.

Gentle Demeanor

Even in a tense situation like the slap game, he maintains a calm and patient attitude. He doesn’t humiliate or ridicule his opponents but instead encourages them to keep trying. This restraint and professionalism could be interpreted as a quiet form of kindness, especially in contrast to the harsh reality of the Squid Game.

Fearless

Last but not least, he is the most fearless man. Even in a life of death situation, he is willing to take the shot and unalive himself so that others could live.
