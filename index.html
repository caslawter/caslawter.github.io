Why Training?

So why have I applied for training first?

I believe that training department is the right choice for me because it aligns with my goals of teaching people. I believe that programming is a crucial skill that everyone should have. However, many people are put off by the intimiditing concepts and syntaxes of programming languages and thats why teaching them can be hard sometimes.

I find the challenge of trying to teach a person fun. Thinking of ways or analogies to dumb down a concept into digestiable content that can be easily understood. And the best part of organising the workshops is to see people having gain new knowledge and apply this new found knowedge to their projects or modules.
